Simple Ways to Reduce Your Emissions

Walk or bike

For short trips, walking or cycling reduces transport emissions and improves health.

Use public transportation

Buses and trains have smaller emissions per passenger than cars.

Eat seasonal & plant-forward

Reducing meat consumption and choosing local produce lowers food-related emissions.

Energy efficiency

Switch to LED lighting, unplug idle devices, and insulate your home.

Compost organic waste

Composting diverts emissions from landfill and creates soil benefits.
