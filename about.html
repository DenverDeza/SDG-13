Why Climate Action Matters

SDG 13 aims to take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts. Target Audience

The primary audience for CarbonTrack includes students and educators seeking hands-on learning tools; households and individuals who want to monitor and reduce their daily emissions; eco-conscious communities and barangay programs aiming to run local sustainability campaigns; NGOs and sustainability advocates who need a simple outreach tool for awareness; and local government units (LGUs) that want an accessible, measurable way to promote community-wide climate action and track progress.
