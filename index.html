Quick Eco Tips

  • Walk or bike for short trips to cut transport emissions.
  • Choose seasonal, plant-forward meals to reduce food emissions.
  • Switch off standby appliances; save energy at home.
  • Use public transport and carpool when possible.
  • Compost organic waste to divert from landfill.
